## üéØ Method 1: Replace Video in YouTube Studio (Recommended)

### Step-by-Step:

1. **Go to YouTube Studio**
   - Visit: https://studio.youtube.com
   - Sign in if needed

2. **Navigate to Your Video**
   - Click **"Content"** in the left sidebar
   - Find your video (the placeholder one)
   - **Click on the video title or thumbnail** to open it

3. **Find "Replace Video" Option**
   - Look for **"Editor"** tab (left sidebar, below "Details")
   - OR look in the main area for options like:
     - "Replace video" button
     - "Video file" section with "Replace" option
     - "Edit video" dropdown menu

4. **Alternative: Check "Editor" Tab**
   - Click **"Editor"** tab (in left sidebar)
   - You should see video timeline/editor
   - Look for **"Replace video"** or **"Change video file"** option
   - Usually at the top or in a dropdown menu

5. **If Not in Editor, Try "Video Details"**
   - Go back to **"Details"** tab
   - Scroll down to find **"Video file"** section
   - There should be a **"Replace"** or **"Upload new version"** button
   - Click it and select your new video file
