### Step 3: Trim & Cut Sections

#### **Method A: Split & Delete (Recommended)**
1. **Watch your video** and pause at the START of a section you want to remove
2. Click on the video in the timeline (select it)
3. Position the playhead at the start of the section to remove
4. Click the **"Split"** button (scissors icon âœ‚ï¸) - or press `Ctrl+B` (Windows) / `Cmd+B` (Mac)
5. Move playhead to the END of the section to remove
6. Click **"Split"** again
7. Click on the section you just split (between the two splits)
8. Press **Delete** key to remove it

#### **Method B: Trim from Ends**
1. Click on the video in timeline
2. Drag the **left edge** inward to trim the beginning
3. Drag the **right edge** inward to trim the end
4. This cuts from the start/end only

### Step 4: Speed Up Transaction Waits (Optional)
1. Select the section where you're waiting for transactions
2. Look for "Speed" or "Duration" option
3. Set speed to 2x or 3x (makes it faster)
4. Or just cut it entirely (better option)

### Step 5: Export Your Video
1. Click **"Export"** button (top right)
2. Settings:
   - **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (or original)
   - **Frame Rate**: 30fps
   - **Quality**: High
   - **Format**: MP4
3. Choose save location
4. Click "Export"
5. Wait for processing (may take a few minutes)
