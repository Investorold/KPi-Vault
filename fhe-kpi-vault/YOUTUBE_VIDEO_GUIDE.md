## Quick Guide - Creating Your Demo Video

### üé¨ Step 1: Record Your Video

#### **Option A: Using Google Chrome/Edge Built-in Recorder** (Easiest)

1. **Open Chrome/Edge Browser**
   - Go to your demo URL: `https://kpi-vault.zamataskhub.com`
   - Or record locally at `http://localhost:4173`

2. **Start Screen Recording**
   - Press `Ctrl+Shift+S` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+Shift+S` (Mac)
   - Or: Open DevTools (`F12`) ‚Üí Click the "..." menu ‚Üí "More tools" ‚Üí "Recorder"
   - Click "Start a new recording"
   - Select "Record with Camera" if you want your face visible

3. **Record Your Demo**
   - Show the app working
   - Explain what you're doing (optional but recommended)
   - Record for 2-5 minutes (enough for submission)

4. **Stop Recording**
   - Click "Stop recording" when done
   - Click "Save" to download the video

#### **Option B: Using OBS Studio** (More Features)

1. **Download OBS Studio** (Free, Professional)
   - Visit: https://obsproject.com/
   - Install for your operating system

2. **Quick Setup**
   - Open OBS Studio
   - Click "+" under "Sources" ‚Üí "Display Capture" ‚Üí Select your screen
   - Click "+" ‚Üí "Audio Input Capture" ‚Üí Select your microphone (optional)
   - Click "Start Recording" (bottom right)
   - Stop when done (video saved in Videos folder)

#### **Option C: Using Built-in Screen Recorder** (Simple)

**Windows:**
- Press `Windows + G` to open Game Bar
- Click the record button (or press `Windows + Alt + R`)
- Record your screen
- Stop recording when done

**Mac:**
- Press `Shift + Command + 5`
- Select "Record Entire Screen" or "Record Selected Portion"
- Click "Record"
- Stop with `Control + Command + Esc`

**Linux:**
```bash
# Install SimpleScreenRecorder
sudo apt-get install simplescreenrecorder

# Or use VLC Media Player
# Media ‚Üí Open Capture Device ‚Üí Capture Device ‚Üí Desktop
```

### üé• Step 3: Upload to YouTube

1. **Go to YouTube**
   - Visit: https://www.youtube.com
   - Sign in with your Google account (create one if needed)

2. **Create Channel** (If First Time)
   - Click your profile picture (top right)
   - Click "Create a channel"
   - Choose a name (can be your name or "Project Demos")
   - Click "Create channel"

3. **Upload Video**
   - Click the camera icon (top right) ‚Üí "Upload video"
   - Or go directly to: https://www.youtube.com/upload

4. **Select Your Video File**
   - Drag & drop your video file
   - Or click "SELECT FILES" and choose your video
   - Wait for upload (may take a few minutes)

5. **Configure Video Settings**
   - **Title**: "FHE KPI Vault - Demo Video" (or similar)
   - **Description**: Add a brief description
   - **Visibility**: Select **"Unlisted"** (Important!)
     - This means only people with the link can see it
     - Your video won't appear in public searches
   - Click "Next" through other steps (tags optional)

6. **Publish**
   - Click "Publish" when ready
   - YouTube will process the video (may take 5-15 minutes)

### üîó Step 4: Get Your Video Link

1. **Once Published**
   - Go to your YouTube Studio: https://studio.youtube.com
   - Click "Content" in left sidebar
   - Find your video

2. **Copy the Link**
   - Click on your video
   - Copy the URL from the address bar
   - Example: `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=abc123xyz`
   - Or click "Share" ‚Üí Copy link

3. **Use This Link in Submission Form**
   - This link will work immediately
   - You can update the video content later (link stays the same)

### ‚ùì Troubleshooting

**"I don't have a Google account"**
- Create one at https://accounts.google.com/signup
- Takes 2 minutes

**"Upload is too slow"**
- Check your internet connection
- Try uploading at off-peak hours
- Be patient, it will finish

**"Video processing taking too long"**
- Normal - can take 5-30 minutes
- You can use the link once it says "Processing complete"

**"How do I update the video later?"**
- Go to YouTube Studio ‚Üí Your video ‚Üí Edit
- You can replace the video file with a new one
- The link URL stays the same!
